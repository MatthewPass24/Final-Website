About Us

Established in 2007 by childhood friends Jake Cherichallo and Matty P, Little Bird Toy Company was founded on a shared reverence for the imaginative adventures of their youth. United by a desire to offer children toys that transcended mere entertainment, Jake and Matty set out to create a company that ignited creativity, fostered active engagement, and nurtured developmental growth. Drawing from their own fond memories of playtime, they envisioned a world where every toy sparked curiosity and every play session became a canvas for boundless imagination. With unwavering dedication to quality craftsmanship, innovative design, and a steadfast commitment to enriching the lives of children, Little Bird Toy Company has soared to prominence, becoming a beacon of joy and inspiration in homes worldwide. Their legacy is one of cherished memories, laughter-filled playtimes, and a lifelong love for the magic of childhood imagination.
